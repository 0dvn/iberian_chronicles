Madrid

Madrid is the capital of Spain. It is a big city, but it is easy to get around. We spent three days there and we always found something new to do.

  • Retiro Park

    This park is right in the middle of the city. People go there to walk, read, or rent a small boat on the lake. We went on a Sunday morning and it was full of families. It is a good place to rest between visits.

  • The Prado Museum

    The Prado is one of the most famous museums in the world. We saw paintings by Goya and Velázquez. It is a big museum, so we only saw part of it. We recommend going in the morning when it is less crowded.

  • Puerta del Sol

    This is the centre of Madrid. There is a famous clock here that people watch on New Year's Eve. Around the square there are many shops and cafés. It is always busy, but it is worth a visit.

  • Travel Tips

    The metro in Madrid is very good. You can get almost anywhere with it. If you are hungry at night, do not worry — many restaurants in Madrid open late. Dinner at 9pm is normal here.

  • Final Thoughts

    We enjoyed Madrid a lot. It is a city that has everything. You can visit museums, walk in parks, eat great food, and stay out late. We will go back.
