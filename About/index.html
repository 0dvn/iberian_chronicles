Welcome to Our Spain Travel Blog

Hello! We are Dina, Darío, and Taha. We love Spain! We started this blog to share our favorite places with you.

Spain is a beautiful country with many things to see. We visit busy cities like Madrid and Barcelona. We also go to quiet villages and sunny beaches. We want to show you the best food and the most beautiful views.

Questions and Answers

1)Why did you start this blog?

We want to help people plan easy and fun trips to Spain. We share what we learn from our own travels. [cite: 23]

2)Who are the team members?

Dina Bakhtiari loves history. Darío Vivancos Novak takes the photos. Taha Jahangiri makes the maps.

3)What can I find on this website?

You can find stories about Spanish food, maps of hidden places, and photos of famous landmarks.

4)Is it hard to travel in Spain?

No! Spain is very friendly. We give you simple tips on how to move around and what to eat.

5)How can I talk to you?

You can go to our Contact page. We have a form there for your questions!
