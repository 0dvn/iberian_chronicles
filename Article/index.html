A Day in Valencia

Valencia is a beautiful city in Spain. It is famous for its history and its food.

  • The Modern City

    We visited the City of Arts and Sciences. The buildings look like they are from the future. They are very white and shiny.

  • Photo Gallery

    Click on the photos to see them better.

    Valencia is the home of Paella. This is a very famous rice dish in Spain. We ate it near the beach!

  • Travel Tips

    If you go to Valencia, you should walk through the Turia Gardens. It is a very long park in an old river. It is perfect for a walk with friends.

  • Final Thoughts

    We think Valencia is a great place to visit. It is not too busy, and the people are very kind.
